EXPECT is an environment for developing knowledge-based systems that includes knowledge acquisition tools to extend and modify knowledge bases. Research topics include knowledge-acquisition, knowledge modeling, description and composition of problem solving knowledge, ontology development and use in a problem-solving context, and distributed web-based problem solving. We have used EXPECT to develop several applications including air campaign planning, workarounds for target damage, transportation planning, and course of action analysis.
EXPECT is being used in the Constable interface.
