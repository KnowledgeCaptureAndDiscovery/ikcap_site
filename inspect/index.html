An Air Campaign Planning Evaluation Aid

Description

Inspect is an air campaign plan critiquing tool. Current funding is supporting thechnology transition to the Joint Defense Planner, which is on the path to becoming an integral part of TBMCS. INSPECT was originally developed under the DARPA Rome Laboratory Planning Initiative, and was demonstrated at the first US Air Force Expeditionary Force Experiment (EFX-98).
